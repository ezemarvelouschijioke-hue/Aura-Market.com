📦 Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) available at checkout. Free shipping on orders over $50.
How can I track my order?
Go to Order Tracking and enter your order number. You'll also receive tracking updates via email.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship within Nigeria only. International shipping coming soon!

🔄 Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?
Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact support to initiate return.
How long do refunds take?
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item. You'll receive confirmation via email.

💳 Payments & Coupons

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Paystack, all major credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are secure and encrypted.
How do I use a coupon code?

🎁 Products & Bundles

What are bundle deals?
Bundle deals let you buy multiple products together at a discounted price. Check our Bundles page for current offers.

Flash sales are limited-time discounts that appear on our homepage. Grab them before the timer runs out!

👤 Account & Rewards

How do I earn rewards points?
Earn 10 points for every $1 spent. Redeem 100 points for $1 off your next purchase. Check your points in the account section.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. We'll send a reset link to your registered email.
